💡 How BrainSprout Works
🌱
Your All-in-One Classroom Engagement Tool
BrainSprout helps K-5 teachers manage their day, support students, and keep energy levels just right — all from one easy dashboard.
⭐
Star Reward Board
Add your students and give or remove stars to recognize good behavior and effort. Click any student card to view their full profile including mood history and notes.
📋
Visual Schedule
Build your daily plan in the Schedule tab using quick templates or custom activities. Your schedule syncs to the Home screen so you can check things off as the day goes.
🎮
Brain Breaks & Activities
12 ready-to-go games, brain breaks, and movement activities sorted by grade level. Today's Brain Break on the Home screen rotates daily and includes step-by-step instructions.
💨
Breathing Exercises
6 guided breathing methods with animated prompts — perfect for transitions, before tests, or when the class needs to calm down. Great for all ages K-5.
⏱
Classroom Timer
A big countdown timer with quick presets (1, 5, 10, 15, 20, 30 min) plus a custom input. A mini version also lives right on the Home screen for quick access.
🔊
Noise Expectations
A large color-coded noise level chart the whole class can see. Tap Silent, Whisper, Partner, Group, or Loud to set the current expectation for the room.
😊
Mood Check-In
Log how each student is feeling — Happy, Calm, Sad, Frustrated, or Worried. Suggestions appear for how to support each mood. History is saved to student profiles.
🫁
Sensory Needs
Track sensory accommodations for individual students or the whole class — Quiet Space, Seat Break, Dim Lights, Fidget Tool, Movement, Visual Cue, and Extra Time.
📝
Teacher Notes
Quickly jot observations about student behavior, moods, or needs. Tag notes to specific students, then view or print them as a Weekly Report for parent-teacher conferences.
🌅
Morning Meeting Question
150 built-in conversation starters that rotate daily. The question changes every day and never repeats until all 150 have been used. Click "New Question" to reshuffle anytime.

📚 Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?
Every new account gets 7 days of full access — no credit card needed. After 7 days, the app switches to the free tier. Upgrade to Pro at any time to keep all features.
What's included in the free plan?
The free plan includes: Star Reward Board, Visual Schedule, Noise Expectations, 1 game, 1 brain break, 1 movement activity, and 2 breathing exercises.
What does Pro include?
Pro ($4.99/mo) unlocks everything: all 12 activities, all 6 breathing methods, Mood Check-In, Sensory Needs tracker, Teacher Notes, Weekly Reports, Student Profiles, and Referral Rewards.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to My Account → Subscription and click "Cancel Subscription". You'll keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period.
I paid but my features are still locked. What do I do?
Go to Tools → scroll to any locked feature → tap "Already subscribed? Tap to unlock". If that doesn't work, contact support at with your account email.
My students' data isn't showing. What happened?
Make sure you're logged in with the same email you used when adding students. If the issue persists, contact us at .

Welcome to BrainSprout!

Here's everything you can do

1
Add Your Students ⭐
Go to the Stars tab and click + Add Student. Add each student's name, grade, and pick a color. You can give or remove stars any time — great for rewarding good behavior!
👧
2
Set Up Today's Schedule 📋
Open the Schedule tab to build your daily plan, or use a quick template. The same schedule shows on your Home screen so you can check things off as the day goes.
🗓
3
Run Brain Breaks & Breathing 🎮
Use the Activities tab for 12 ready-to-go games and movement breaks. The Breathing tab has 6 calming methods — great for transitions or when energy runs high.
🧠
4
Use the Timer & Noise Level ⏱
The Timer tab counts down for any activity. The Noise tab shows a big color-coded chart your whole class can see — tap a bar to set the expected noise level.
🔊
5
Track Moods & Sensory Needs 😊
In the Tools tab, tap a student's name then select their mood or active sensory needs. A summary shows every student's status at a glance so nothing slips through.
🫁
6
Write Teacher Notes 📝
Jot quick observations in the Notes section — they save to your account automatically so you can look back any time, edit them, or delete them.
